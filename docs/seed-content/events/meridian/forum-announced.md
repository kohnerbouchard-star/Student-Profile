Open the Meridian arc, establish shared facts, activate introductory briefings, and make the first contract available without applying major economic shocks.

## Trigger

One of:

- scenario schedule;
- instructor activation;
- approved automatic story activation.

Requirements:

- active game session;
- no terminal Meridian outcome;
- event not already active or resolved in the session.

## Player-facing report

Lumenor has invited all ten countries to Starfall to consider the Meridian Corridor, a shared infrastructure proposal linking trade, energy, rail, data, customs, commodities, and research systems.

No agreement has been reached. Countries are preparing proposals and conditions.

## Fact status

Confirmed official announcement.

## Immediate effects

- introduce Meridian global briefing;
- initialize country perspectives;
- send interaction from Ila Meren;
- make `contract.meridian.evaluate-corridor.v1` available;
- optionally create neutral market attention without direct price shock.
