## Purpose

Create a water-allocation and energy-resilience decision before a full drought or utility failure occurs.

## Trigger requirements

- Meridian arc active;
- reservoir outlook below seasonal expectation;
- no contradictory high-reservoir recovery event;
- simultaneous-warning limit respected.

## Player-facing report

Valerion’s Water and Energy Commission reports reservoir levels below seasonal expectations. Current service remains stable, but export guarantees and peak demand could reduce flexibility if dry conditions continue.

## Fact status

Confirmed reservoir measurement plus forecast risk.

## Affected entities

- Valerion clean-energy utility;
- water-infrastructure company;
- households and regional users;
- energy-importing Meridian partners;
- green-finance and reputation outlook.

## Immediate effects

Semantic directions:

- hydropower outlook: moderate negative;
- alternative energy and efficiency demand: mild positive;
- domestic confidence: minor negative;
- export reliability: minor to moderate negative;
- green-infrastructure demand: mild positive.

## Decision options

1. Reduce export guarantees.
2. purchase temporary energy.
3. accelerate alternative clean-energy investment.
4. introduce conservation and targeted pricing.
5. maintain commitments and accept higher risk.

## Delayed effects

- reduced exports affect partners and revenue;
- imports increase cost and external dependence;
- investment creates debt and delayed resilience;
- conservation affects users differently;
- ignored warning raises later outage or rationing risk without guaranteeing it.

## Recovery

- improved weather;
- diversified generation;
- lower demand;
- successful conservation;
- regional energy-sharing agreement;
- infrastructure upgrade.

## Contract and interaction hooks

- Elia Varen warning;
- water-allocation analysis;
- energy-option contract;
- disruption-response contract.

## Failure and expiry

The warning resolves when conditions normalize or escalates through a distinct drought or outage event. It must not remain permanently active.

## Validation

- current service and future risk distinguished;
- drought not guaranteed;
- household and export effects both visible;
- conservation is not costless;
- recovery paths exist;
- event session scoped and bounded.
