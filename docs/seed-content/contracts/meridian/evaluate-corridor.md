# Evaluate the Meridian Corridor

Stable content ID: `contract.meridian.evaluate-corridor.v1`
Content type: contract definition
Version: 1.0.0-draft
Maturity: draft
Family: onboarding and analysis
Scope: global; assigned or available within a game session
Story arc: `story-arc.global.meridian-corridor.v1`
Stage: forum announced
Implementation status: field mapping pending

## Player-facing summary

Review the proposed Meridian Corridor and identify its major benefits, costs, dependencies, and unresolved risks.

## Objective

Create a structured assessment showing why the Corridor could improve trade and resilience, and why its financing, ownership, data, environmental, and capacity choices matter.

## Required output

A short written or structured response containing:

1. three potential benefits;
2. three material risks;
3. at least four affected countries or institutions;
4. one short-term trade-off;
5. one long-term trade-off;
6. one question that must be answered before approval.

## Evidence requirements

Use:

- the global briefing;
- at least two country profiles;
- one institution proposal or warning;
- one economic concept from the glossary.

## Learning objectives

- identify interdependence;
- distinguish benefit from distribution of benefit;
- recognize infrastructure as a public and private economic asset;
- identify uncertainty before decision-making.

## Eligibility

- game session active;
- Meridian arc introduced;
- player authenticated;
- no prior approved completion of the same non-repeatable contract in the session.

Country assignment is not required, but assigned-country context should be shown when available.

## Acceptance and lifecycle

- may be assigned automatically or made globally available;
- one active attempt per player per session;
- manual or rubric-based approval;
- changes may be requested;
- reward issues only after approval;
- expiration follows the Stage 1 decision window or instructor policy.

## Completion rubric

Approved when the response:

- identifies both benefits and costs;
- includes multiple countries or institutions;
- contains no major factual contradiction with the briefing;
- distinguishes current fact from forecast;
- provides an actionable unresolved question.

Changes requested when:

- answer is one-sided;
- risks are generic and unrelated to the Corridor;
- required evidence is absent;
- forecast is written as confirmed fact.

## Reward policy

Reward band: onboarding-standard.

Final amount: pending combined reward-economy review.

Possible nonmonetary outcomes:

- contract-chain progression;
- access to the country-exposure contract;
- introductory reputation or achievement progress if supported.

No item reward is required for the pilot.

## Narrative effects

On approval:

- marks the player as briefed for the arc;
- unlocks `contract.meridian.analyze-country-exposure.v1`;
- may influence tutorial completion;
- does not select a Corridor model.

## Failure and expiry

Rejection should allow revision unless the session window closes.

Expiry should not penalize balances. It may close the guided chain while leaving later global content available according to instructor policy.

## Admin guidance

Reviewers should evaluate reasoning and required evidence, not whether the player selected a preferred political or economic conclusion.

## Validation

- reward not hard-coded before review;
- all referenced briefings exist;
- approval is idempotent;
- no duplicate payout;
- narrative unlock applies once;
- Player and Admin status labels map to authoritative lifecycle.
