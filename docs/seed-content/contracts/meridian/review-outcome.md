# Review the Meridian Outcome

Stable content ID: `contract.meridian.review-outcome.v1`
Content type: contract definition
Version: 1.0.0-draft
Maturity: draft
Family: reflection and evaluation
Scope: player within a game session
Story arc: `story-arc.global.meridian-corridor.v1`
Stage: resolution or follow-up
Implementation status: field mapping pending

## Player-facing summary

Evaluate what changed after the Meridian decision and whether the final structure improved resilience, growth, fairness, and trust.

## Objective

Use the actual session outcome—not the expected outcome—to compare immediate results, delayed consequences, winners, costs, and unresolved risks.

## Required output

1. Identify the final Corridor resolution.
2. Identify one immediate positive result.
3. Identify one immediate cost or lost opportunity.
4. Identify one delayed or continuing consequence.
5. Name one country, company, institution, or group that benefited.
6. Name one that bears additional risk or cost.
7. Compare the result with the player’s earlier recommendation.
8. Propose one revision for the follow-up phase.

## Learning objectives

- evaluate policy using evidence;
- distinguish predicted and observed outcomes;
- identify distributional consequences;
- revise a position;
- understand unintended effects.

## Eligibility

- Meridian arc has a terminal or provisional resolution;
- player completed at least one prior Meridian contract, unless instructor assigns the reflection independently;
- outcome data available.

## Completion rubric

Approved when:

- response uses the actual session resolution;
- evidence distinguishes immediate and delayed effects;
- beneficiaries and costs are specific;
- earlier recommendation is examined honestly;
- revision responds to an observed weakness.

Changes requested when:

- response describes a resolution that did not occur;
- all effects are presented as positive or negative;
- the player rewrites the earlier recommendation rather than comparing it;
- revision is unrelated to evidence.

## Reward policy

Reward band: standard reflection or chain-completion.

Final amount: pending economy review.

Potential nonmonetary result:

- complete the Meridian pilot chain;
- achievement progress;
- unlock follow-up arc briefing;
- narrative reputation if supported.

## Narrative effects

This contract records learning and may select or support follow-up content. It should not retroactively change the resolution.

## Failure and expiry

Revision allowed while outcome data remains accessible.

If the game session closes, instructor policy determines whether late reflection is accepted. No reward is issued twice if the session is reopened or the submission is retried.

## Admin guidance

Reward evidence-based revision. Do not require the player to claim their original recommendation was wrong; a valid response may conclude that it was appropriate but incomplete.

## Validation

- actual resolution derived from authoritative session state;
- prior submissions referenced without exposing private data to other players;
- chain completion applied once;
- reward issuance idempotent;
- follow-up unlock references valid content;
- closed-session behavior defined.
