## Headline

Starfall Forum Opens Talks on Shared Meridian Corridor

## Summary

Representatives from all ten Econovarian countries have been invited to Lumenor to discuss a shared infrastructure network linking ports, rail, energy, customs, payments, data, commodities, and research systems.

No country has approved final participation terms.

## Article

The Starfall Meridian Forum opened its first formal session today, beginning negotiations over a proposed shared infrastructure system known as the Meridian Corridor.

The proposal would connect several parts of Econovaria’s economy that currently operate through separate national or regional systems. Supporters say better connections could reduce trade delays, improve emergency routing, create industrial demand, and make customs and payment records more reliable.

The proposal does not yet have a final route, financing structure, ownership model, or security plan.

Xalvorian institutions are expected to present large-scale financing options. Yrethian institutions will focus on ports, customs, insurance, and trade standards. Dravenlok and Northreach are expected to emphasize industrial capacity and strategic supply. Lumenor has proposed shared governance and oversight. Solvend and Syndalis are preparing technical and security proposals.

Eldoran and Valerion are reviewing whether increased Corridor commitments could place pressure on domestic food, energy, and water resilience.

The Forum has stated that participation in the talks does not commit a country to the final project.
