## Correction notice

An earlier public statement or report suggested that a specific country, institution, or operator caused the Meridian records disruption.

The available investigation record does not support that conclusion.

The Starfall Public Media Trust withdraws the attribution claim. The confirmed facts remain that selected cargo and payment records conflicted and that trade verification was disrupted.

## What changed

- the claimed cause or responsible party is no longer reported as confirmed.

Not changed:

Attribution can influence:

- diplomatic relationships;
- sanctions or access decisions;
- company reputation;
- market confidence;
- public trust;
- the fairness of emergency responses.

A correction does not mean the disruption was unimportant. It means the evidence does not justify the earlier attribution.
