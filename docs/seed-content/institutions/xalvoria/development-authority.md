## Purpose

Structure, finance, and supervise strategic infrastructure investments inside Xalvoria and across participating countries.

## Mandate

- evaluate major projects;
- propose debt, equity, and mixed financing;
- negotiate collateral and ownership terms;
- appoint project-management structures;
- monitor repayment and construction milestones;
- restructure eligible projects.

## Authority limits

The Authority cannot unilaterally transfer foreign public assets, override borrower law, control monetary policy, or guarantee national budgets.

## Economic role

Converts accumulated capital and project expertise into infrastructure, fee income, interest income, strategic access, and political influence.

## Core tension

Development speed and financial return versus debt concentration, ownership influence, transparency, and borrower autonomy.

## Meridian role

Proposes the finance-first model for `story-arc.global.meridian-corridor.v1`.

Lead character: `character.xalvoria.cassian-rhyl.v1`.

## Contract authority

May issue:

- financing comparison;
- debt-sustainability analysis;
- project-risk review;
- term-sheet negotiation;
- restructuring recommendation.

## Event hooks

- financing offer;
- project completion;
- cost overrun;
- borrower default;
- restructuring;
- disclosure investigation;
- capital withdrawal.

## Trust conditions

Trust rises through transparent terms, realistic forecasts, shared risk, and successful completion. Trust falls through concealed control clauses, conflicts of interest, repeated overruns, or coercive restructuring.

## Failure behavior

Project failure should activate restructuring, write-down, delay, or transfer disputes according to approved mechanics. It must not delete prior ledger or market history.

## Validation

- distinguish sovereign narrative finance from player banking;
- no legal ownership transfer is implied without supported mechanics;
- all returns have risk and timing;
- default effects are bounded and recoverable;
- institution cannot directly manipulate player holdings.
