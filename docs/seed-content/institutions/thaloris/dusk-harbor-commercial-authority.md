## Purpose

Coordinate port access, bonded zones, emergency routing, repair permits, and commercial capacity in Dusk Harbor.

## Mandate

- allocate berths and bonded capacity;
- license repair and salvage operations;
- propose emergency routes;
- coordinate cargo documentation with domestic authorities;
- negotiate recognition with foreign ports and insurers;
- publish capacity and operating notices.

## Authority limits

The Authority does not control all private warehouse operators, determine foreign sanctions, guarantee insurance, or adjudicate criminal liability.

## Economic role

Makes otherwise stranded or delayed trade commercially usable through flexible routing, repair, refurbishment, and warehousing.

## Core tension

Commercial speed and inclusion versus verification, reputation, and insurability.

## Meridian role

Offers overflow and emergency routing in `story-arc.global.meridian-corridor.v1`.

Lead character: `character.thaloris.tovan-rell.v1`.

## Contract authority

May issue:

- alternate-route analysis;
- repair-capacity allocation;
- bonded-warehouse audit preparation;
- emergency cargo plan;
- Yrethia recognition negotiation.

## Event hooks

- overflow routing request;
- bonded-capacity expansion;
- cargo-record discrepancy;
- repair-yard accident;
- insurer withdrawal;
- recognition agreement;
- port blacklisting threat.

## Trust conditions

Trust rises with verifiable records, safety improvement, and successful emergency delivery. Trust falls through concealed cargo, selective enforcement, corruption, or repeated preventable accidents.

## Failure behavior

Loss of Corridor status should leave non-Corridor repair and regional trade available. Recovery can involve audit, operator replacement, safety investment, or limited-route certification.

## Validation

- distinguish informal, unregulated, noncompliant, and illegal activity;
- avoid mechanics that reward sanctions evasion;
- audit events preserve allegation and confirmation states;
- port availability changes are session scoped;
- private operator conduct is not automatically attributed to the whole country.
