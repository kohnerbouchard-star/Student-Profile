## Purpose

Govern strategic mineral licensing, export review, reserves, and foreign access to nationally sensitive resource infrastructure.

## Mandate

- license strategic extraction;
- review export commitments;
- maintain reserve policy;
- negotiate resource-access agreements;
- coordinate with Frostgate logistics authorities;
- publish supply assessments within security limits.

## Authority limits

The Office cannot independently approve foreign ownership, national budgets, labor agreements, or environmental exemptions. Those require separate authorized institutions.

## Economic role

Balances revenue from exports against domestic processing, security, reserve levels, and long-term bargaining power.

## Core tension

Investment and market access versus sovereignty and supply security.

## Meridian role

Negotiates mineral and energy commitments for `story-arc.global.meridian-corridor.v1`.

Lead character: `character.northreach.darek-voss.v1`.

## Contract authority

May issue:

- mineral-demand analysis;
- reserve-policy comparison;
- foreign-investment term review;
- domestic-processing evaluation;
- strategic supply negotiation preparation.

## Event hooks

- export review;
- mineral demand surge;
- reserve warning;
- foreign-ownership proposal;
- mining interruption;
- processing investment;
- sanctions or access restriction.

## Trust and legitimacy

Trust rises when the Office publishes understandable rules, respects safety and environmental review, and avoids arbitrary export changes. Trust falls when restrictions appear politically selective or agreements conceal control terms.

## Failure behavior

A failed agreement should reduce access or delay supply rather than erase resources or permanently disable Northreach. Recovery may use renegotiation, diversified partners, or domestic processing investment.

## Validation

- all resource effects reference approved indicators or event metadata;
- no tactical security information in player copy;
- foreign-ownership decisions reference authorized approval paths;
- export changes are session scoped;
- no implied control over player balances or company holdings.
