## Purpose

Coordinate training, retention, recruitment, career development, and workforce forecasting for specialized research and engineering roles.

## Mandate

- publish skill-gap assessments;
- coordinate education and apprenticeship programs;
- recommend retention and recruitment measures;
- evaluate project staffing assumptions;
- support international qualification recognition.

## Authority limits

The Council cannot set company wages, approve immigration status by itself, control research ownership, or compel workers to remain in Solvend.

## Economic role

Maintains the human-capital pipeline required for Solvend’s research, aerospace, software, and precision-engineering economy.

## Core tension

Fast project staffing versus sustainable training, worker mobility, living costs, and research quality.

## Contract and event hooks

- skilled-talent constraint;
- retention package;
- apprenticeship expansion;
- international recruitment;
- project-scope review.

## Trust conditions

Trust rises through accurate role-specific forecasting, funded programs, and transparent outcomes. Trust falls through inflated shortage claims, unfunded promises, or treating workers as interchangeable capacity.

## Validation

- recruitment and mobility remain fictional and non-stereotyped;
- workforce data is aggregated;
- Council authority remains advisory and coordinating;
- training benefits require time;
- no private employee or player data appears.
