## Purpose

Represent farmers, agricultural producers, storage operators, and member-owned processing groups in harvest, pricing, reserve, export, transport, and investment decisions.

## Mandate

- publish member production and cost evidence;
- coordinate shared storage, purchasing, and transport proposals;
- represent producer interests during reserve and export review;
- recommend investment in resilience and productivity;
- participate in outcome and policy reviews.

## Authority limits

The Cooperative cannot set national export policy, release government reserves, set national prices, approve subsidies, or compel nonmember producers.

## Economic role

Provides distributed production capacity and information while helping smaller producers coordinate market access, storage, inputs, and investment.

## Core tension

Producer income and future production incentives versus household affordability, reserve protection, and export obligations.

## Economic interests

- stable and transparent prices;
- affordable inputs and credit;
- reliable rail, warehouse, and port capacity;
- predictable export rules;
- investment in storage, water, equipment, and risk management;
- protection from policies that transfer the entire cost of a supply shock to producers.

## Vulnerabilities

- lower harvest volume;
- high input and transport cost;
- sudden export restriction;
- weak storage and spoilage control;
- credit pressure;
- unequal bargaining power between small producers and large buyers.

## Contract and event hooks

- Eldoran harvest revision;
- reserve release consultation;
- export renegotiation;
- storage and rail investment;
- targeted producer support;
- next-season recovery.

## Trust conditions

Trust rises through representative member evidence, transparent cost reporting, and willingness to acknowledge household effects.

Trust falls through inflated shortage claims, concealed inventory, capture by large producers, or opposition to every affordability measure.

## Relationship notes

- works with the Commodity Stability Board on production evidence;
- negotiates with the Consumer Price Council over distributional effects;
- depends on Eldoran rail, warehouses, and Yrethian or Thalorian routes;
- may support targeted household assistance rather than broad price suppression;
- may oppose open-ended export bans.

## Interaction directions

- producer response to harvest revision;
- reserve-release consultation;
- export and price proposal;
- storage-investment request;
- review of whether policy preserved next-season production.

## Validation

- the Cooperative does not represent every producer automatically;
- producer benefit and household cost remain separate;
- no private farm, player, or inventory data appears;
- no unsupported authority over prices, reserves, or exports;
- production evidence remains session scoped when used in gameplay;
- institution does not guarantee that higher prices create higher total producer income.
