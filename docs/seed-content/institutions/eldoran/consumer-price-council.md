## Purpose

Represent household affordability, essential-goods access, and distributional effects in food, reserve, transport, tax, subsidy, and import decisions.

## Mandate

- publish household price-impact analysis;
- recommend targeted support designs;
- review eligibility and administrative access;
- identify unequal regional burdens;
- participate in policy outcome reviews.

## Authority limits

The Council cannot set retail prices, release reserves, approve subsidies, restrict exports, or access private player balances.

## Economic role

Improves policy design by revealing how aggregate price, growth, or supply changes affect households differently.

## Core tension

Rapid and broad relief versus fiscal cost, targeting complexity, producer incentives, and long-term supply.

## Contract and event hooks

- harvest revision;
- household-support proposal;
- reserve release review;
- food-price communication;
- recovery evaluation.

## Trust conditions

Trust rises through transparent methods, current data, and practical eligibility design. Trust falls through unsupported claims, hidden distributional assumptions, or programs inaccessible to intended recipients.

## Validation

- household data remains aggregated;
- no private player financial information;
- Council authority remains advisory;
- affordability concerns have economic weight;
- support options include administrative and fiscal cost.
