## Player promise

Syndalis offers opportunity through cybersecurity, fintech, payment systems, data infrastructure, digital services, and the networks that make modern trade possible.

The player arrives because Meridian integration requires identity verification, payment routing, customs data, network resilience, and technical support.

Syndalis rewards skill and speed. It also lives under constant tension between security, privacy, trust, and concentrated information power.

## Arrival scene

The player arrives at Blacklight Transit Exchange, where physical immigration processing is linked to digital identity and payment registration.

Visible signals:

- companies recruiting security and data workers;
- public terminals explaining digital-residency requirements;
- premium technology districts beside older service neighborhoods;
- advertisements for rapid fintech careers;
- civil-rights notices challenging excessive data collection.

Immediate need:

Complete account and identity verification without surrendering optional personal information the player does not fully understand.
