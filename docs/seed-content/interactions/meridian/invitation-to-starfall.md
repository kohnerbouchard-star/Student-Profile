## Purpose

Introduce the Meridian proposal and allow the player to express an initial participation stance without treating the response as a binding national commitment.

## Opening message

“The Starfall Meridian Forum has opened its first session. The proposal is ambitious, but no country has agreed to final terms. Before negotiations begin, the Forum is collecting initial priorities and concerns from each delegation.”

## Response options

### A. Support immediate negotiations

Player text:

“Begin negotiations now. Delaying the discussion will increase infrastructure and trade costs.”

Strategic interpretation:

Speed and participation.

Immediate reply:

“Your delegation will be recorded as supporting immediate negotiations. Participation does not imply acceptance of any financing or governance model.”

Effects:

- narrative: participation stance recorded if supported;
- unlock: introductory contract;
- reputation: mild Forum cooperation if supported;
- economic: none.

### B. Request economic analysis first

Player text:

“Provide more information about costs, ownership, and country exposure before negotiations advance.”

Strategic interpretation:

Evidence and due diligence.

Immediate reply:

“The Forum will provide the shared assessment materials. The request may slow initial positioning but improves the record available to delegates.”

Effects:

- unlock: introductory analysis contract;
- narrative: due-diligence stance;
- economic: none.

### C. Prioritize national conditions

Player text:

“Our country will participate only after its own resource, capacity, and security conditions are defined.”

Strategic interpretation:

Conditional participation.

Immediate reply:

“Your delegation will be listed as participating conditionally. Country-specific requirements will be requested in the next stage.”

Effects:

- unlock: country exposure contract after prerequisite;
- narrative: conditional stance;
- economic: none.

### D. Decline an initial commitment

Player text:

“Observe the Forum, but do not record support for the Corridor at this stage.”

Strategic interpretation:

Caution and optionality.

Immediate reply:

“The Forum will record observer status. You may review the proposal and enter negotiations later while the participation window remains open.”

Effects:

- narrative: observer stance;
- introductory analysis remains available;
- no penalty;
- later participation may require an additional step.

## No-response behavior

Default to “briefing delivered; no stance recorded.” Do not interpret silence as rejection or support.

## Expiry

Closes when competing-model negotiations begin. The introductory contract may remain available according to scenario policy.

## Validation

- response is not a binding national treaty;
- no monetary or market effect;
- no-response neutral;
- available once per player or delegation per session;
- response ownership derived from authenticated session;
- Forum reply matches option selected.
