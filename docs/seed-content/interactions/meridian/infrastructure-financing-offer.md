## Purpose

Present a credible finance-first offer with real speed and funding benefits, while exposing control, concentration, debt, and disclosure costs.

## Opening message

“The Corridor can be financed now. Delay is not neutral; it increases construction cost and leaves existing weaknesses in place. Xalvoria is prepared to commit capital if responsibility and downside protection are equally clear.”

## Response options

### A. Accept debt with strategic-asset protections

- Benefit: fast capital and lower initial coordination burden.
- Cost: repayment and control clauses.
- Reply: Cassian provides staged-disbursement and review terms; final national approval remains required.

### B. Remove asset-control clauses and pay a higher rate

- Benefit: stronger borrower autonomy.
- Cost: higher financing expense and stricter affordability test.
- Reply: Cassian accepts the structure if debt service remains credible.

### C. Convert part of the package to shared equity

- Benefit: shares risk and reduces fixed repayment.
- Cost: complex ownership and governance.
- Reply: Cassian requests clear voting, exit, and profit-allocation rules.

### D. Split financing among several institutions

- Benefit: diversification and reduced Xalvorian control.
- Cost: slower negotiation and fragmented accountability.
- Reply: Cassian remains willing to lead one tranche but warns about coordination delay.

### E. Decline the package

- Benefit: avoids immediate dependency.
- Cost: uncertain funding, delay, and lost scale.
- Reply: Cassian withdraws the comprehensive offer while leaving bilateral proposals possible.

## No-response behavior

The offer remains pending until expiry; no debt or ownership is created.

## Expiry

Closes at financing resolution or specified offer deadline.

## Validation

- no interaction itself creates debt or transfers assets;
- sovereign narrative finance distinct from player banking;
- each option contains a cost;
- Cassian is not written as secretly malicious;
- no-response creates no obligation;
- response session scoped and auditable.
