## Purpose

Present industrial employment, procurement certainty, modernization, safety, component dependency, and competitive cost as linked trade-offs.

## Opening message

“Dravenlok can supply rail, steel, vehicles, and heavy equipment at the scale the Corridor requires. We will not reserve that capacity without credible orders, and credible orders require a schedule.”

## Response options

### A. Guaranteed purchase volumes

- Benefit: lower unit cost, capacity reservation, and employment stability.
- Cost: reduced flexibility and risk of paying for obsolete or delayed output.
- Reply: Mara requests a minimum order, delivery schedule, and adjustment clause.

### B. Competitive procurement with modernization support

- Benefit: preserves competition while helping Dravenlok improve capacity.
- Cost: higher complexity and no guaranteed volume.
- Reply: Mara accepts if modernization support is committed before bidding.

### C. Joint production with Solvend components

- Benefit: combines scale and advanced systems.
- Cost: coordination, intellectual-property, and import dependency.
- Reply: Mara requests technology-transfer and supply-continuity terms.

### D. Smaller guaranteed tranche

- Benefit: tests delivery and limits exposure.
- Cost: weaker economies of scale and slower employment gains.
- Reply: Mara accepts a pilot but warns that pricing will reflect lower volume.

### E. Open procurement without guarantees

- Benefit: maximum buyer flexibility.
- Cost: Dravenlok may not reserve capacity or modernize in advance.
- Reply: Mara leaves Dravenlok firms free to bid without national capacity commitment.

## No-response behavior

No capacity is reserved and no guarantee is created.

## Expiry

Closes when procurement strategy is finalized or production windows are allocated elsewhere.

## Validation

- Ministry does not bind private firms without supported authority;
- guarantees have volume and adjustment terms;
- employment benefit includes debt and capacity risk;
- no tactical defense content;
- no-response neutral;
- response session scoped.
