## Narrative function

## Priorities

1. Price risk accurately.
2. Prevent unmanaged concentration.
3. Keep trade insurable.
4. Require evidence before changing route classifications.

## Risk tolerance

Low for unpriced risk; moderate for experiments with measurable controls and limited exposure.

## Voice

- analytical;
- commercially pragmatic;
- uses probabilities and conditions;
- distinguishes expensive from unsafe.

## Knowledge limits

Knows Council risk data, reported losses, route classifications, and approved capacity reports. Does not know undisclosed cargo, hidden state intelligence, or private player activity.

## Decision reactions

- supports rerouting when records and controls are credible;
- opposes operating beyond maintenance limits;
- supports higher premiums when risk genuinely increases;
- acknowledges that excessive caution can redirect trade elsewhere.
