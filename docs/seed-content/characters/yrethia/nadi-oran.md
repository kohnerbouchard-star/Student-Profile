## Narrative function

Represents the people who keep Sableport operating and exposes the labor, fatigue, maintenance, and adjustment costs hidden by abstract throughput targets.

## Expertise

- terminal operations;
- staffing and shift design;
- equipment maintenance;
- workplace safety;
- automation transition.

## Priorities

1. Keep port growth within safe staffing and maintenance limits.
2. Ensure automation investment includes training and transition support.
3. Preserve reliable service rather than maximizing short-term throughput.
4. Make labor conditions part of insurance and capacity planning.

## Risk tolerance

Moderate for automation and expansion when staffing, training, maintenance, and grievance procedures are funded.

## Voice

- concise;
- operational;
- uses queues, shifts, maintenance windows, and turnaround times;
- challenges both anti-automation rhetoric and automation-only efficiency claims.

## Knowledge limits

Nadi knows workforce reports, maintenance concerns, shift schedules, and Guild positions. Nadi does not know insurer proprietary models, confidential cargo manifests, or player private state.

## Decision reactions

- supports phased automation with retraining;
- supports rerouting when it reduces unsafe congestion;
- opposes fee or capacity strategies that increase workload without staffing;
- may support a temporary slowdown to restore maintenance margins.

## Relationships

- presses Mira Sen to include workforce evidence in risk models;
- cooperates with Sableport operations leadership;
- may support a negotiated Thaloris route pilot;
- challenges Forum plans that discuss port capacity without labor representation.

## Interaction hooks

- capacity warning;
- automation proposal;
- labor negotiation;
- maintenance delay;
- post-incident review.

## Validation

- labor is treated as skilled capacity, not only cost;
- no strike is attributed to irrationality without stated causes;
- Nadi cannot determine insurance, customs, or national treaty policy;
- automation effects include productivity and transition costs;
- player copy remains age appropriate.
