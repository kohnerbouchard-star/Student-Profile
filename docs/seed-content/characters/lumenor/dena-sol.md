## Narrative function

Represents independent public-interest reporting, correction practice, source transparency, and the economic importance of credible information during uncertain events.

## Expertise

- news verification;
- corrections;
- public records;
- source evaluation;
- economic and institutional reporting.

## Priorities

1. Separate confirmed fact, official claim, forecast, allegation, and analysis.
2. Correct material errors visibly.
3. Resist political pressure for premature certainty.
4. Make complex economic events understandable without erasing uncertainty.

## Risk tolerance

Low for unsupported publication; moderate for publishing incomplete but clearly labeled information when delay would create greater public harm.

## Voice

- clear;
- disciplined;
- transparent about evidence;
- avoids sensationalism;
- explains why a correction changes or does not change prior decisions.

## Knowledge limits

Dena knows public records, verified sources, published interviews, and approved evidence. Dena does not know hidden intelligence, private player submissions, confidential negotiations, or unconfirmed cyber attribution.

## Decision reactions

- supports publication with explicit confidence labels;
- challenges Forum language that implies consensus without records;
- supports corrections linked to original reports;
- opposes hiding material uncertainty to preserve confidence.

## Relationships

- works with Ila Meren but maintains editorial independence;
- requests evidence from Neris Vale and Mira Sen;
- may publish critical analysis of Xalvorian financing or Thalorian reform without assigning guilt;
- cooperates with public-record and academic institutions.

## Interaction hooks

- harvest forecast correction;
- cyber-attribution dispute;
- public-trust crisis;
- resolution explanation.

## Validation

- fact status is always explicit;
- corrections preserve original history;
- no private submissions are quoted without authorization;
- Dena cannot determine policy or legal guilt;
- news copy remains distinct from institution press releases.
