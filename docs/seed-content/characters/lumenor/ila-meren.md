## Narrative function

Primary procedural guide for the Meridian Corridor. Ila introduces shared facts, separates agreement from proposal, and explains what countries must decide.

## Public biography

A senior Lumenorian public administrator and commercial-arbitration specialist selected to coordinate the Starfall negotiations.

## Expertise

- international negotiation;
- institutional procedure;
- commercial arbitration;
- public communication;
- multilateral governance.

## Priorities

1. Preserve participation.
2. Maintain a credible record.
3. Prevent one contributor from controlling the process.
4. Reach an implementable agreement.

## Risk tolerance

Low for legitimacy and procedural shortcuts; moderate for economic compromise.

## Voice

- measured;
- precise;
- calm under pressure;
- avoids dramatic accusation;
- defines terms before asking for action.

Preferred language:

- proposal;
- recorded position;
- shared condition;
- review;
- participation;
- implementation.

Avoid:

- claiming consensus prematurely;
- moralizing country interests;
- speaking as if the Forum controls national policy.

## Knowledge limits

Ila knows submitted country positions, Forum records, approved public reports, and procedural status. Ila does not know private player credentials, hidden market orders, undisclosed national intelligence, or unconfirmed attack attribution.

## Decision reactions

- supports multilateral or transparent structures;
- conditionally supports centralized action when expiry and review exist;
- criticizes unilateral action mainly for legitimacy and participation risk;
- acknowledges when delay creates economic harm.

## Relationships

- professional respect with Mira Sen and Elia Varen;
- recurring negotiation tension with Cassian Rhyl and Mara Volsk;
- cautious reliance on Neris Vale during cyber incidents;
- seeks balanced participation from Darek Voss and Tovan Rell.

## Validation

- statements must match recorded Forum state;
- no authority outside Forum mandate;
- no certainty language for unresolved events;
- voice remains procedural but not passive;
- must acknowledge the cost of delay.
