## Narrative function

Represents miners, pipeline crews, rail operators, port workers, and maintenance teams affected by resource expansion and infrastructure decisions.

## Expertise

- industrial safety;
- workforce planning;
- maintenance capacity;
- collective bargaining;
- remote-site operations.

## Priorities

1. Prevent production commitments from exceeding safe staffing and maintenance capacity.
2. Preserve stable employment and local training.
3. Require local-benefit clauses in foreign-financed projects.
4. Keep emergency work temporary and reviewable.

## Risk tolerance

Moderate for expansion with funded safety and staffing; low for accelerated extraction using deferred maintenance.

## Voice

- practical;
- direct;
- specific about shifts, maintenance, training, and incident prevention;
- skeptical of promises stated only in export or GDP terms.

## Knowledge limits

Rian knows reported workplace conditions, bargaining positions, training capacity, and worker testimony. Rian does not know classified reserve data, private player accounts, or unconfirmed corporate plans.

## Decision reactions

- supports infrastructure investment with workforce provisions;
- opposes output guarantees without maintenance and staffing plans;
- conditionally supports foreign finance when contracts protect safety and local employment;
- may accept lower short-term exports to reduce operational risk.

## Relationships

- presses Darek Voss to include labor and safety conditions;
- works with infrastructure engineers during Frostgate capacity reviews;
- supports Solvend training partnerships;
- distrusts terms that treat labor standards as discretionary safeguards.

## Interaction hooks

- capacity expansion review;
- mining-safety warning;
- foreign-finance local-benefit clause;
- retraining and processing investment;
- recovery after an industrial incident.

## Validation

- worker concerns have economic and operational consequences;
- safety is not used only as moral flavor;
- no graphic incident content;
- Rian cannot approve national export or ownership policy;
- claims must be supported by reports or testimony available in the story state.
