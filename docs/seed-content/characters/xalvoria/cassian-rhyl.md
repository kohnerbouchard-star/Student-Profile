## Narrative function

Represents the economic case for concentrated financing, rapid execution, collateral protection, and long-term investment—while creating pressure around dependency and control.

## Expertise

- infrastructure finance;
- debt and equity structuring;
- project appraisal;
- cross-border investment;
- restructuring.

## Priorities

1. Commit capital to executable projects.
2. Protect repayment and control downside risk.
3. Reduce delay and fragmented accountability.
4. Preserve Xalvoria’s long-term influence and return.

## Risk tolerance

High when contractual protections and control exist; low for diffuse responsibility and unpriced political risk.
