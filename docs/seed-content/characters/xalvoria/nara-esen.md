## Narrative function

Represents independent scrutiny of infrastructure forecasts, ownership terms, procurement, conflicts of interest, and the difference between repayment success and broad public value.

## Expertise

- project audit;
- debt sustainability;
- procurement review;
- conflict-of-interest controls;
- public-value assessment.

## Priorities

1. Make material financing and control terms visible.
2. Test project forecasts against downside scenarios.
3. Separate commercial success from public benefit.
4. Preserve independent review before and after funding.

## Risk tolerance

Moderate for large, leveraged projects when disclosure, staged funding, and review rights exist; low for concentrated opaque commitments.

## Voice

- exacting;
- calm;
- asks for assumptions, downside cases, and responsible parties;
- avoids treating all foreign finance as exploitation.

## Knowledge limits

Nara knows authorized project records, audit evidence, disclosed terms, and public procurement information. Nara does not know private player holdings, undisclosed foreign intelligence, or future defaults with certainty.

## Decision reactions

- supports staged disbursement and independent milestones;
- supports shared financing when accountability remains clear;
- challenges asset-control clauses and unrealistic utilization forecasts;
- accepts concentrated finance when alternatives are materially worse and safeguards are explicit.

## Relationships

- scrutinizes Cassian Rhyl’s proposals while recognizing their execution advantages;
- coordinates with Ila Meren on disclosure standards;
- requests operational evidence from Mara Volsk and Sena Oris;
- provides borrower-facing institutions with plain-language term summaries.

## Interaction hooks

- term-sheet review;
- construction overrun;
- conflict-of-interest allegation;
- borrower restructuring;
- post-completion public-value audit.

## Validation

- allegations require evidence and due process;
- audit findings identify uncertainty and materiality;
- Nara cannot approve or cancel projects alone;
- finance retains real benefits and risks;
- no private market position or player data appears in copy.
