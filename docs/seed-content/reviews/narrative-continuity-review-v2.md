# Narrative Continuity and Choice Review v2

Review status: changes still required
Supersedes for current status: `narrative-continuity-review-v1.md`
Scope reviewed: revised Meridian arc, deterministic resolution model, ten interaction files, events, contracts, institutions, and lead characters

## Executive finding

Iteration 2 closes the two largest pilot blockers. The Meridian outcome is now reproducible from declared conditions, and all ten lead interactions have standalone response trees with no-response and expiry behavior. The pilot remains short of full production approval because supporting perspectives, cross-arc concurrency, chronology labels, cancellation detail, and full news records are incomplete.
